How the Sequence Works
1
Practice 3 sessions per day — morning, after lunch, and evening.
2
Start with 10–15 questions per session; ramp up to 20 over the weeks.
3
Add each new subject to your rotation as it is covered in class.
4
From Week 4 on: your first daily session is always do-overs of missed questions from 1–2 weeks prior.
5
Use secondary MBE sources every few days to stay sharp and avoid look-alike fatigue.
6
Missing a day? Make it up before or after by increasing session volume — don't skip it.
Why This Matters

California scales its written section scores to MBE performance. A stronger collective MBE result means a higher pass rate for everyone — not just you. This sequence is built on that principle.
